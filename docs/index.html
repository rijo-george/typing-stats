How It Works

Stupid simple.
By design.

1

Launch and type

Open Typing Stats once. It sits in your menu bar and quietly counts every keystroke and word. No setup, no configuration.

2

Watch your stats grow

Click the menu bar icon anytime to see live WPM, word count, keystroke count, and the 24-hour activity chart. Your desktop buddy evolves as you type faster.

3

Share your daily card

At the end of the day, generate a beautiful summary card with fun comparisons. Share it on social media or just admire your output.
